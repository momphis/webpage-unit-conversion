Units Test

Change the display with the drop-down at the bottom right.

When converted units are displayed along with the author's units as in the "SI", "Imperial", and "All" options they are displayed in parentheses. Alternatively, the "SI Only" and "Imperial Only" options substitute the converted units for the author's units as appropriate. These display options are specified entirely in the CSS source.

See the JavaScript source for more detailed documentation important caveats.

With units elided

I walked … carrying… at … until I was … above sea level where it was a chilly … which is about … lower than in the valley.

With SI units

I walked 5 km carrying 20 kg at 2 1/2-3 kph until I was 380.5 m above sea level where it was a chilly 10 centigrade which is about 5 deg C lower than in the valley.

With Imperial units

I walked 3 miles carrying 45 lbs at between 1.5 and 2 mph until I was 1250 ft above sea level where it was a chilly 50 Fahrenheit which is about 9°F lower than in the valley.
